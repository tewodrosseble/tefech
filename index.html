About TeffTech

TeffTech is a pioneering startup focused on providing comprehensive services related to the teff plant. Founded by Tewodros Seble, our company aims to revolutionize the teff industry through innovative solutions and dedicated support for farmers and businesses alike.

Our Services

  • Disease Assessment: We offer expert assessment and management strategies to protect your teff crops from diseases.
  • Plant Varieties: Discover a wide range of teff plant varieties, each suited for different climates and soil types.
  • Buying and Selling: Our marketplace connects buyers and sellers of teff, ensuring fair prices and quality products.

Teff Plant Varieties

TeffTech offers a diverse selection of teff varieties, including traditional and hybrid options. Our experts can help you choose the best variety for your needs, whether you're looking to maximize yield, improve disease resistance, or adapt to specific growing conditions.

Marketplace

Our online marketplace is designed to facilitate the buying and selling of teff. Whether you're a farmer looking to sell your harvest or a business seeking quality teff products, our platform ensures secure and efficient transactions.
